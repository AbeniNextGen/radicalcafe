An Inspiring Space Built For Sodo

Radical Cafe is a premium hybrid space designed equally for deep work sessions and lively social meetups. We source the finest local Ethiopian beans, roasting them to perfection to fuel the innovations, exams, and collaborations of Sodo's university students and professionals.

Bold, rich, and pulled to perfection. Crafted entirely from premium, locally sourced Sidamo & Yirgacheffe beans.

Cold Brews & Teas

Slow-steeped refreshing cold brews and crisp, vibrant local herbal teas perfect for an afternoon pick-me-up.
